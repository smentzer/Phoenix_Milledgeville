The Haven

If you are looking for the perfect place to live independently while still being conveniently located to Georgia College and Georgia Military- look no further than The Haven. These townhomes offer 2-bedroom, 2.5- bathroom, and 3-bedroom, 3.5- bathroom options for, perfect for upper classmen and graduate students with their own style, and furniture.

Residents of The Haven enjoy the luxury amenities offered by our partner communities, Prospect A and B.

house

Prospect A&B

Phoenix Milledgeville’s Prospect communities are divided into two different locations to meet the needs of any Georgia College or Georgia Military student, and all within walking distance.

Prospect A offers unbelievably spacious 3-bedroom, 3.5- bathroom, and 4- bedroom, 4-bathroom fully furnished apartments with modern, stainless-steel appliances, and a full-size washer and dryer.

Prospect B has even more options with 3-bedroom, 3- bathroom, 3-bedroom, 3.5- bathroom, and 4- bedroom, 4-bathroom fully furnished apartments, with a spacious living room and a beautiful, modern kitchen. No matter which Prospect community you decide to live with you will enjoy the luxury amenities inclusive of a beautiful clubhouse, grill area, resort-style pool, 24-hour gym, and lounge area, all in your backyard.

house

Campus Edge

Campus Edge offers beautiful, spacious 2-, 3-, and 4-bedroom apartments, conveniently located within walking distance to both Georgia College and Georgia Military. All apartments come fully furnished and loaded with modern, stainless-steel appliances.

Residents of Campus Edge have access to the luxury amenities offered at our partnered Prospect Communities.

Phoenix Milledgeville offers everything you need to thrive in college, all within walking distance to both Georgia College and Georgia Military. With four different locations, Prospect A & B, Campus Edge, and The Haven, Phoenix Milledgeville provides you the most variety in Milledgeville, GA. All of our communities have access to our luxury amenities, beautiful clubhouses, and extraordinary management staff.

Our Mission is to manage and develop beautiful communities that support the personal and educational growth of the residents we serve, through celebrating diversity, striving for excellence, and leading with integrity.
